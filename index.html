Pigeon-Tech Feature Overview

Pigeon-Tech transforms traditional visitor journeys into interactive, data-driven experiences. Designed for modern museums, zoos, and theme parks, the platform offers a suite of features that enhance engagement, improve operations, and deliver real-time insights.

Exhibit Spotlight

Automatically recognizes exhibits and displays detailed stories, images, and videos.

Navigation

Smart wayfinding that guides visitors across galleries, exhibits, amenities, and exits.

Heat Maps

Visualize visitor density and movement patterns to optimize exhibit layouts.

Ride Updates

Keep visitors informed with real-time ride availability, wait times, and safety alerts.

Tour Planner

Create personalized visit plans based on time, interests, and visitor profiles.

Mobile Food Ordering

Let visitors browse menus and order food from their phones with ease.

Polls & Surveys

Get real-time visitor feedback through in-app polls and surveys.

White Labeling

Customize the entire platform to reflect your museum’s brand identity.

AI-Powered Visitor Experience

Pigeon-Tech is an AI-powered solution that reimagines how attractions connect with their guests. By turning passive visits into interactive journeys, our platform makes every experience smarter, more personalized, and deeply engaging—driven entirely by artificial intelligence.
